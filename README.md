## 📋 What is contractor?

Contractor keeps your construction and home renovation projects organized. You can track your expenses, manage your material lists, and schedule tasks in one place. Whether you are a professional builder or a homeowner working on a kitchen remodel, this tool simplifies your daily planning.

## 💻 System requirements

You do not need special hardware to run this software. Ensure your computer meets these basic needs:

* Windows 10 or Windows 11.
* At least 4 gigabytes of memory.
* A stable internet connection for the initial download.
* Enough storage space for your project files.

## 🚀 Getting started

Once the installation finishes, you will see a shortcut on your desktop. Double-click the icon to open the program.

### Create your first project

1. Click the "New Project" button in the top left corner.
2. Enter the name of your renovation or construction job.
3. Pick a location for your save files on your local drive.
4. Click "Create" to build your project folder.

### Add your tasks

Use the task list to track what you need to finish each day. Click the "Add Task" button at the bottom of the screen. Type the task name, pick a due date, and assign a priority level. The software automatically moves overdue tasks to the top of your list so you never miss a deadline.

### Track your budget

Click the "Expenses" tab to manage your spending. Log each purchase as you go. You can attach digital copies of receipts to each entry. The software calculates your remaining budget in real time to keep your finances in check.

## 🔍 How to use the features

The interface uses standard menus similar to other Windows programs. You can navigate between your dashboard, project lists, and financial reports using the sidebar on the left.

### Managing material lists

You must track your lumber, paint, and hardware to avoid multiple trips to the store. Open the "Materials" tab to add your shopping list. You can update quantities as you use materials on-site. If you run low on a specific item, the system highlights that row so you know to restock.

### Generating reports

Click the "Reports" button to see your progress. You can export these reports as PDF files to share with clients or partners. The reports summarize your completed work and current spending.

## 🛡️ Privacy and data

Your data stays on your computer. Contractor stores all project information in local files. The software does not send your personal documents or financial records to external servers. You maintain control over your information at all times. If you need to back up your projects, copy your project folder to an external hard drive or a cloud storage service of your choice.

## ❓ Frequently asked questions

### Will this software work on older versions of Windows?
This program requires modern features found in Windows 10 and 11. It will not work on Windows 7 or 8.

### Can I run multiple projects at the same time?
Yes. You can switch between projects using the "File" menu. The program keeps each project separate to prevent data overlap.

### How do I update the software?
Check the repository page occasionally for a new version. You can download the new setup file and run it directly over your current installation to receive the latest updates.

### Does the software require an internet connection?
You only need the internet to download the initial installer and to download new updates. All other features work without a connection.

### Can I share my project folder with team members?
Since the files live on your computer, you can move your project folder to a shared network drive or use a file syncing service. Just ensure only one person opens the project at a time to prevent file conflicts.

3. Check that your antivirus software is not blocking the installation.
4. Reinstall the program using the official download link provided at the top of this document.
